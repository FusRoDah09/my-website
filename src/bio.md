# Bio

Hi I'm Darpan. I'm currently working in my family business. Previously, I founded Alai, an apparel brand. Before that, I was a consultant with Analysys Mason, where I worked on multiple projects across technology, media, and telecom sectors in the Middle East, Eastern Europe, Central Asia, and Southeast Asia. I hold an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad and a BA (IPM) from the Indian Institute of Management Indore.
